Krishna Kills Kamsa

Kamsa, the tyrannical king of Mathura, was the cousin of Devaki, the mother of Lord Krishna. Upon learning of Krishna’s birth, Kamsa sent Asuras to kill him, but Krishna defeated them all. Eventually, Kamsa made a cunning plan to kill Krishna with the help of the gigantic wrestler Chanura. Krishna beat Chanura and confronted Kamsa, holding him by his hair and pulling him down. Kamsa lost balance and fell backward, after which Krishna beheaded him with his sword. This marked the end of Kamsa’s cruel rule and the liberation of Mathura’s people.
